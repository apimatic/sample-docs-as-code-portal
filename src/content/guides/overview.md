### Questions API Leaders Actually Ask

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="How fast can we actually launch this?">
Think hours, not quarters. Most teams go from OpenAPI spec to live portal in under 30 minutes. Connect your spec, customize your look, and deploy. That's it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens when our API evolves?">
Your portal evolves with it, automatically. When your spec changes, your documentation updates instantly. No manual work, no version mismatches, no stale examples confusing developers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can developers test our secure endpoints?">
Absolutely. The testing console handles API keys, OAuth, bearer tokens – whatever your API requires. Developers get the full experience, safely sandboxed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do we need to manage hosting and infrastructure?">
Zero infrastructure on your end. We handle hosting, scaling, security, and uptime. You get a custom domain that looks completely yours, without the operational overhead.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Which programming languages get SDKs?">
Python, JavaScript, Java, C#, PHP, Ruby, Go, Swift, Kotlin, and growing. If your developers use it, we probably generate for it. New languages added based on developer demand.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How much can we customize the design?">
Everything. Your colors, typography, logo, layout – the portal becomes an extension of your brand. Developers should feel like they never left your ecosystem.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why does AI-readiness matter for our API?">
Because the future of development is AI-assisted. When developers ask coding assistants about integration options, your API will be discoverable and recommendable. It's like SEO, but for AI.
